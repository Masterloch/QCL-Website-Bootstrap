About QCL

Quality Chemical Laboratories (QCL) serves the pharmaceutical industry. We provide expert scientific services in support of drug products, drug substances, in-process materials and raw materials in all phases of Research, Development, and Commercialization.

QCL is known for responsiveness to customer needs, flexiblity in our approach, and essential project communication from scientist to scientist.

Areas of expertise include:

Analytical development and validation, sample analysis, residual solvents analysis, synthetic chemistry, compendial testing, wet chemistry, trace metals analysis, mass spectroscopy, microbiology, and stability management services.

We recently expanded our R&D formulation development group beyond prototype development, optimization of solid dosage forms, and are preparing to bring GMP manufacturing for clinical trial materials.

Our Custom Synthesis capabilities set us apart. Both R&D and GMP synthesis are available in a milligram to kilogram scale. Impurity identification, isolation, and synthesis are core competencies. Reference standards are made for industry customers in a cost effective manner as well as API process development and optimization.

Our business strategy has been consistent from the company’s inception in 1998. Hire a mix of talented scientists and support staff, enable them to focus on science and on-time delivery while maintaining a high level of quality output. The result of our philosophy has created both customer loyalty and sustainable company growth.

Give us a call today to discuss your project requirements. We would be happy to host you for a QCL site visit or audit when the time is right. Thank you.
